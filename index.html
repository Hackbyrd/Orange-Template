Our Story

A living room overflowing with Asian groceries may not sound like the birth of a technology company, but that is where it all began.

  • September 2013

    Our Humble Beginnings

    Our founder, Christy Chen, was determined to prove that Asian groceries could be delivered across the country quickly and affordably, bringing the taste of home to customers' doors anywhere in the United States.

  • January 2014

    Goodbye 401K, Hello Startup

    In order to prove her vision, Christy quit her job as a software engineer and single-handedly built a revolutionary demand management system from the ground up. She then went to the store, bought Asian groceries, and began shipping them to customers from her own home.

  • September 2014

    Partner In Crime Found

    The technology was proven and the customers were there, but Christy knew that a startup journey cannot be taken alone. So she reached out to an old friend, Neama. He was about to graduate from business school and was looking for his next great adventure. So they talked and brainstormed, argued and planned, and soon enough, Neama was convinced and committed himself to OrangeNow Technologies, and the partnership was off and running.

  • Today

    Expansion

    Today, the team at OrangeNow Technologies is growing by leaps and bounds, as we strive to use technology to make the lives of our customers better every single day. Christy continues to be passionate about helping others and serving as a role model for young, female entrepreneurs everywhere. And it all started from a living room overflowing with Asian groceries.

Founding Team

Christiana Chen

Founder and Chief Technology Officer

Christy is software engineer and demand management expert, and has developed OrangeNow’s proprietary demand management system from the ground up. Previously, she was a senior technology software consultant at Apptio and a software developer at Boeing. Christy has an MS in Information Systems from University of Washington and a BA in Management from the University of Hawaii. She has a passion for entrepreneurship, women in technology, and Taiwanese culture, and is a frequent guest speaker at events related to these topics.

Neama Dadkhahnikoo

Co-founder Chief Executive Officer

Neama is a serial entrepreneur with significant experience in startups, both as founder and as a business consultant where he’s raised $2.6 million in funding for early-stage companies. Previously, he was the CEO of Nikoo Energy (a cleantech startup), principle partner at Bruin MBA Consulting, and a software developer for Boeing. Neama has an MBA in technology and entrepreneurship from UCLA Anderson and a BA in Math and Computer Science, with a minor in Physics, from UC Berkeley. A frequent mentor to startups and young entrepreneurs at UCLA Anderson, Neama always makes time to pay it forward.
